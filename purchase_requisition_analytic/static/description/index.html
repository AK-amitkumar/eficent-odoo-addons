Project cost purchase planning

Record analytic planned costs associated to purchase orders

Eficent brings you this module to automatically record the analytic planned costs associated to purchase orders.

This module defines a new structure to record planned and commited costs of the projects and contracts.

You can create purchase orders out of the planned costs.
